The Chore Organizer

Never Argue Over Whose Turn It Is

Do you ever have arguments over which person is suppose to take out the trash? Is "I did it last time!" is just not convincing enough? Use the Chore Organizer to quickly create your apartment and tasks to keep track of who is charge of what!

Creating an apartment is quick and easy. You'll be able to finish the setup process in just a few minutes and be on your way to a more organized life.

Assign Tasks and Rotate

The Chore Organizer lets roommates log into their apartment and check off which tasks they have finished. It will automatically assign the following roommate to that task.

If you're unsure of what tasks you currently have, simply log into your apartment to quickly see!
